How to play Mairi — the simple version

Mairi is a Force-casting support and battlefield controller. As a Way of the Sage Consular, her primary role is keeping allies alive with her boosted healing, while threatening enemies with utility powers and dark-side damage. She has more combat presence than a typical healer — Force Imbuement turns her Lightblade into a WIS-based weapon for free, making her a real threat in melee when the Force Points need conserving.

Every fight starts with a resource check: Force Points (8/16 max) are everything. Healing costs FP. Damage costs FP. Force Shield (reaction, 2/LR) is her main defense. Force Recovery (1/LR) gets FP back mid-fight. Her Disciple of Life passive makes every heal punch well above its cost — a 1st level Heal restores 1d8+7 total. She's a Miraluka — blindsight 30ft, can't be blinded.

Force Imbuement is free — use it every fight. Bonus action, no FP, lasts a minute. Her Lightblade becomes a +6 attack, 1d8+4 damage WIS weapon. On turns where she isn't casting a leveled power, attacking with an imbued blade or throwing it via Saber Throw costs nothing. The key tension is her bonus action — Imbuement, Force Leap, and Force Whisper all compete for it. Imbue on the first turn before combat gets complicated, then it's free for the rest of the fight.

// Combat Protocol
Turn 1: Bonus action — Force Imbuement (free, lasts 1 min). Then Action: Force Push/Pull or attack. Stay 30-60ft back when casting. Use Heal reactively — don't cast it before an ally needs it. When FP are low: attack with the imbued blade or Saber Throw instead of spending FP. Use Pyrokinesis into clusters with Careful Power to protect allies (+1 FP). Use Dark Side Tendrils for area control and restrain. Use Saber Reflect (reaction) when hit by ranged attacks. Save Force Shield for a hit that would drop you. Use Force Leap (BA) to reposition — but remember it burns your bonus action, so you can't Imbue that turn. Use Seeking Power (+1 FP) when you can't afford to miss.

Key Class Features
Reaction Force Shield 2 / Long Rest
When you are hit by an attack — use your reaction to shield yourself
When a creature hits you with an attack, you can use your reaction to add your proficiency bonus (+2) to your AC for that attack, potentially turning a hit into a miss. Can be used after seeing the roll.
Use when a hit would drop you low or knock you out. You can see the roll first — only use it if it will actually flip the hit to a miss.
Special Force Recovery 1 / Long Rest
Regain Force Points equal to half your Consular level (minimum 1) as a special action
Once per long rest, Mairi can recover Force Points mid-fight. At level 3 this recovers up to 1-2 FP back — small but potentially crucial.
Use at 3-4 FP remaining, before spending down to zero. Never waste it when FP are full.
Way of the Sage · Passive Disciple of Life Always Active
Healing powers use WIS or CHA · +2 + power level extra HP on every heal
Whenever Mairi casts a force power of 1st level or higher that restores HP, the target regains extra HP equal to 2 + the power's level. A 1st level Heal already restores 1d8+4 — Disciple of Life adds +3 on top of that. Her healing is meaningfully stronger than baseline.
Always active — just cast Heal and the bonus applies automatically. Prioritize spending 1st level FP on Heal when an ally is in trouble; the bonus makes it very efficient.
Force-Empowered Casting · Costs FP Careful Power Spend FP when casting
Spend 1 FP when casting a power — choose a number of creatures up to WIS modifier to auto-succeed their saving throw
When casting an area power like Pyrokinesis or Dark Side Tendrils, spend 1 FP to protect up to 4 friendly creatures — they automatically succeed their saving throw and take no damage.
Use when casting AoE powers near allies. Pyrokinesis in a group? Spend 1 FP and allies are safe.
Force-Empowered Casting · Costs FP Seeking Power Spend FP after missing
After missing with a force attack or a target succeeds a save — spend 1 FP to reroll and take the higher result
Can be used even if another Force-Empowered Casting was already used this cast. A safety net for when Saber Throw or Force Disarm misses on a critical moment.
Use when missing would waste a significant FP investment — especially on Saber Throw or a leveled power cast against a key target.
Miraluka · Passive Force Sight Always Active
Blindsight to 30ft — perceive the world through the Force, not eyes
Mairi perceives everything within 30ft through the Force regardless of light conditions. She cannot be blinded within this range. Beyond 30ft she uses normal (Force-enhanced) senses. Invisible creatures within 30ft are still detected.
Always active — remind the GM when enemies try to blind you or fight in darkness. Within 30ft you always see them.
Background · Action Empathic Passive
Sense emotional state of nearby creatures through the Force
Mairi can read the surface emotions and intentions of nearby creatures. Useful for detecting deception, ambushes, or hidden hostility — especially combined with her high Insight (+6) and Animal Handling (+4).
Use in social encounters and before entering unknown situations. Tell the GM you're reading the room.
Force Powers — DC 14 · Attack +6 · WIS +4 · Refreshes on Long Rest
Force Disarm
Innate · Action · 30ft · STR 14
Innate Universal
Force a creature to drop a held item. STR save DC 14 — on fail, item drops. No FP cost on innate use.
Force Push / Pull
Innate · Action · 60ft · STR 14 · 1d4 damage
Innate Universal
Push or pull a creature up to 10ft. STR save DC 14. On fail — creature moved and takes 1d4 kinetic. Great for repositioning dangerous melee enemies away from allies.
Pyrokinesis
Innate · Action · 60ft · 5ft cube · DEX 14 · 1d8
Innate Dark Side
Create a burst of fire in a 5ft cube. DEX save DC 14 — fail takes 1d8 fire. Use Careful Power (+1 FP) to protect allies in the area.
Saber Reflect
Innate · Reaction · Self · Atk +6
Innate Universal
When hit by a ranged attack — reflect it back. Roll +6 to hit. Your main defensive reaction when Force Shield is expended.
Saber Throw
Innate · Action · 30ft · Atk +6 · 1d8
Innate Universal
Hurl your lightblade — attack +6, 1d8 energy. Returns to hand. Your best ranged damage option when not casting leveled powers.
Force Whisper
Innate · Action · 120ft · No FP
Innate Universal
Send a silent telepathic message to any creature within 120ft. Essential for silent coordination mid-combat.
Spare the Dying
Innate · Action · Touch · No FP
Innate Light Side
Stabilize a creature at 0 HP — no rolls, no FP cost. Use on downed allies immediately to prevent death saves spiraling.
Heal
1st Level · Action · Touch · 2 FP · 1d8+4
1st Level Light Side
Restore 1d8+4 HP + Disciple of Life bonus (+3) = up to 1d8+7 total. Primary healing tool. Costs 2 FP.
Telepathic Link
1st Level · Action · Touch · 2 FP
1st Level Universal
Establish a telepathic connection with one creature. Utility and coordination — best used outside combat or at start of a mission.
Dark Side Tendrils
1st Level · Action · Self (10ft) · STR 12 · 2d6 · 2 FP
1st Level Dark Side
Dark side tendrils erupt in a 10ft emanation around you. STR save DC 12 — fail: 2d6 damage and restrained until end of their next turn. Powerful area control but requires being in the middle of enemies. Use Careful Power to protect adjacent allies.
Stun Droid
2nd Level · Action · 60ft · CON 14 · 4 damage · 3 FP
2nd Level Universal
Target a droid — CON save DC 14 or take 4 damage and be stunned. Highly situational — devastating against droids, useless otherwise. Know when the campaign calls for it.
Turn Combos
// STANDARD TURN — Control + Pressure
Action: Force Push/Pull (STR save DC 14) → Fail: enemy moved + 1d4 damage → Bonus: Force Whisper to coordinate allies
// IMBUED BLADE — Free WIS Melee Damage
Bonus: Force Imbuement (free, no FP) → Lightblade now uses WIS +4 for atk + dmg → Action: Attack at +6, damage 1d8+4 → Lasts 1 minute — no FP spent
// IMBUED BLADE + SABER THROW — Range or Melee Same Setup
Bonus: Force Imbuement (imbue blade) → Action: Saber Throw (+6, 1d8 energy, 30ft) → Blade returns to hand → Imbue still active next turn
// REPOSITION + IMBUED STRIKE — Leap In, Hit Hard
Bonus: Force Leap (reposition up to range) → Action: Imbued Lightblade attack (+6, 1d8+4) → Note: BA already used — no Imbuement this turn → Imbue on a prior turn for best results
// SETUP + CONTROL — Disarm Then Press
Action: Force Disarm (STR save DC 14) → Fail: enemy drops weapon → Ally moves in — enemy unarmed and threatened → Bonus: Force Whisper alert to the opening
// SUPPORT TURN — Emergency Heal
Ally at 0 HP: Action: Spare the Dying (no FP) → Stabilised — no death saves this turn → Next turn: Heal (2 FP) → 1d8+7 HP restored
// SAFE AoE — Pyrokinesis Without Friendly Fire
Careful Power (1 FP) — up to 4 allies auto-succeed → Action: Pyrokinesis (DEX save DC 14, 1d8 fire) → Enemies burn, allies untouched
// DARK ZONE — Tendrils + Careful Power
Move into cluster (Force Leap if needed) → Careful Power (1 FP, protect adjacent allies) → Action: Dark Side Tendrils (2d6, STR save, restrain) → Restrained enemies: disadvantage on attacks, can't move
// DEFENSE CHAIN — Reflect + Shield
Enemy fires ranged attack → Reaction: Saber Reflect (+6) — redirect it back → If a second hit threatens: Force Shield (2/LR) → +2 AC — potentially flips the hit to a miss
// CLUTCH MISS — Seeking Power Safety Net
Cast Saber Throw or leveled power → Miss / target succeeds save → Spend 1 FP: Seeking Power — reroll, take higher → Can stack with Careful Power on same cast
